FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

Do I need an appointment?

No, walk-ins are always welcome. We serve clients on a first-come, first-served basis. If you prefer a specific time, calling ahead is recommended.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash and all major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express.

How long does a typical manicure or pedicure take?

A classic manicure takes about 30 minutes. A classic pedicure runs about 45 minutes. Spa and deluxe services may take longer depending on the treatment.

Do you offer services for kids?

Yes, we offer kid-friendly manicure and pedicure services at reduced prices. Children under 10 must be accompanied by an adult.

Are your tools sanitized between clients?

Absolutely. All metal tools are sterilized in an autoclave, and we use fresh disposable files, buffers, and liners for every single client. Your safety is our priority.

Where are you located?

We are located at 370 Garrisonville Rd, Suite 105, Stafford, VA 22554. Right on the main Garrisonville corridor with plenty of parking available.
